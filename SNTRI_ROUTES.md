## Schedule Information
- **Operating Days**: Most routes run daily (7 days/week)
- **Desert routes**: Monday-Saturday only
- **Peak Season**: Routes may have additional departures in summer
- **Advance Booking**: Recommended for long-distance routes (Tunis-Sfax, Tunis-Gabes)

## Bus Fleet
- **Standard Intercity**: 45-55 seats, A/C, WiFi
- **Express**: 48-55 seats, A/C, WiFi, wheelchair accessible
- **City Buses**: 35 seats, A/C

## Pricing Notes
All prices listed are in **Tunisian Dinar (TND)**
- Approximate USD conversion: 1 TND ≈ 0.32 USD (rates vary)
- Children under 6: Free
- Children 6-12: 50% discount
- Students: 25% discount (with valid ID)
- Seniors (65+): 20% discount
